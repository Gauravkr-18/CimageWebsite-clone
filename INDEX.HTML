BCA admission 2024 and BBA admission 2024 started at CIMAGE College Patna. Other courses like BBM, BBA, B.com and PGDM admission are also started for 2024-27 session. At CIMAGE, admission is granted through ‘Entrance cum Scholarship Test‘, followed by ‘One to one’ Personal Interview. Those, who qualify the mentioned rounds below, are given the chance to take admission at CIMAGE. To get admission for 2024-27 session you can call at 7250767676 or fill the enquiry form at our website www.cimage.in. The counsellors will get back to you and provides you all the information regarding admission in BCA, BBA, BBM, BSc.IT or PGDM courses for 2024 session. Economical weaker student can opt the facility of Student credit card issues by Bihar government to get admission at CIMAGE group of Institution. It is one of the best college comes under Aryabhatta Knowledge University and Patliputra University. For the admission of BCA and BBA courses, CIMAGE College is the best college as it is offering world class and modern education environment with campus placement facility. Getting admission in CIMAGE is the best opportunity for a student who wants to make a good career.

Since the CIMAGE college has started, it is always a first choice of all the students for the admission of BCA and BBA in Patna. Once the student get admission in CIMAGE, he feels like entering a new world of modern education. Along with the main course syllabus we are providing extra add-on courses to make our students Job ready.

Candidates seeking admission in Undergraduate Programs BCA/ BBA / BBM / B.Com(P) / B.Sc.(IT) may either apply Online or may collect ‘Entrance Exam Form’ from the College Reception by paying Rs. 800/-

Eligibility Criteria:


  1. The Applicant must have qualified Intermediate/+2 in Arts, Science, or Commerce as a regular student or as a student of distance education
  2. The Applicant must have obtained at least 45% marks overall in intermediate/+2 level. The students from SC/ST/EBC/Differently abled Categories should have scored 40% marks overall.
